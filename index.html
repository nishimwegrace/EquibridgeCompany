About Us

Empowering Lives Through Compassion

At EQUIBRIDGE CARE Support Services, we believe that everyone deserves to live a fulfilling and independent life. Our mission is to provide high-quality, person-centered support that enables our clients to achieve their goals and thrive in their local community.

With a team of experienced and dedicated support workers, we bring a friendly and warm approach to every individual we serve, ensuring their safety, comfort, and happiness.
